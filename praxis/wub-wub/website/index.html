What it does

The signature pump.
Finally yours.

Most sidechain plugins give you two knobs and hope for the best. Wub Wub gives you a canvas. Draw the exact shape of the ducking effect — and every kick fires it precisely.

✏️
Draw Your Envelope
A Catmull-Rom spline editor lets you place, drag, and remove control points to design any ducking curve imaginable. No knobs. Just shape.
Catmull-Rom · 64-pt LUT
👁️
See What Triggers
A full-width sidechain meter shows the exact envelope follower level compared against your threshold. Drag the white line directly on the meter to set it.
Envelope follower · Per-sample
⚡
Audio & Sync Triggers
Trigger from audio level detection or lock to the DAW's playhead at any beat division. Use whichever fits your session — no compromise.
Audio sidechain · Tempo sync
🎨
7 Preset Shapes
Linear, Expo, Smooth, SmoothExp, FastAtk, SlowRel, DblPeak — one click to load a starting point, then tweak it to exactly what you hear in your head.
Presets · Fully editable
🔇
Click-Free by Design
A 2ms IIR gain smoother eliminates the transient click that appears when gain jumps instantaneously at trigger time. Toggle it off if you want the raw edge.
IIR smoother · 2ms τ
🎛️
Reactive Mix Knob
The knob flashes white on every trigger and dims when mix is reduced — because you should be able to see the ducking, not just hear it.
Canvas · rAF · Visual feedback

Trigger Modes

Trigger it your way.

AUDIO
A per-sample envelope follower monitors the sidechain input. When the smoothed level crosses your threshold, the curve fires. Hysteresis and a 120ms holdoff prevent false retriggering on the kick's tail.
Envelope follower · 3ms attack · 150ms release · 25% hysteresis
SYNC
Locks the envelope to the DAW's playhead position at a chosen beat division — 1/8, 1/4, 1/2, or 1/1. Guaranteed to stay on the grid regardless of audio dynamics or tempo.
Tempo sync · 1/8 · 1/4 · 1/2 · 1/1 note · Any BPM
